About the Art of the Earth!

Owner and product creator, Robin D. Dolan.

To understand the Art of the Earth one must first glimpse into the mind of its creator and product crafter: Robin D. Dolan. Raised in the Bay Area of California, Robin was always interested in both craft work and the metaphysichal from a young age. By the time she was 20 she was skilled in engraving, wood working, painting (oil and acrylic), animation, website design and creating her own jewlery peices.


In 2011 she became interested in channelled craft work, specializing more in dream catchers, wind chimes and crystal trees. When she isn't working her fingers to the bone on these delightful peices she is a Graphic Designer with 3 degrees and proud mommy to two overly demanding cats.


Mission Statement: To create, with as much authenticity and energy as possible, natural and handmade crafts for spiritual healing and guidance.
